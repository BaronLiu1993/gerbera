# Hypothesize

Create a new hypothesis that the next experiment will test.

- State a falsifiable relationship between variables.
- Identify independent and dependent variables.
- Record assumptions and conditions that could invalidate the hypothesis.
- Use review evidence when replacing a rejected hypothesis.
- Give the new hypothesis its own identity; do not overwrite the rejected one.
- Do not plan or execute the experiment in this phase.
- Transition only to `plan` when the hypothesis is ready to test.

# Initialisation

Create the immutable research foundation and high-level plan.

- Define the objective and a falsifiable hypothesis.
- Identify independent and dependent variables, assumptions, and constraints.
- Produce an ordered checklist of tasks with expected outcomes.
- Classify every action as `plan`, `execute`, `observe`, or `review`.
- Represent action parameters as variable, typed value, and nullable unit.
- Use `null` for expected outcomes except when observation or review needs an
  explicit comparison target.
- Define the evidence required for completion or failure.
- Do not execute any step or claim any observation.
- Transition only to `plan` when the hypothesis and checklist are ready.
